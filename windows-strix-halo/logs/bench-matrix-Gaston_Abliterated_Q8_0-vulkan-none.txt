If the bandwidth is insufficient, the GPU cores sit idle, drastically reducing throughput regardless of their theoretical floating-point capacity.

To mitigate this, quantization reduces the precision of model weights, typically from 16-bit or 32-bit floats to 4-bit or 8-bit integers.
